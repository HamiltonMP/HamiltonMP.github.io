简介 / BIOGRAPHYBIOGRAPHY

孟庆鹏，来自中国江苏的自由摄影师，现于美国学习与创作。曾入选 Leica Fotografie International（LFI）Master Shot，并获 IPA 国际摄影比赛 Official Selection。他的实践聚焦于纪实与城市语境，关注多背景下的个体经验与现实片段并尝试在日常生活之中捕捉安静与冲突的元素。目前其正构思一项横跨中美两地的纪实摄影项目。

Qingpeng Meng is a freelance photographer from Jiangsu, China, currently based in the U.S. for his academic and creative pursuits. Recognized as a Leica Fotografie International (LFI) Master Shot and an IPA Official Selection. His practice focuses on individual experiences and realistic fragments across diverse backgrounds. Currently developing a project spanning China and the U.S.
